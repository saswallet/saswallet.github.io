1. How does the SAS Wallet work?

SAS Wallet gives you an interface to interact with various crypto Blockchains for sending, receiving and storing coins and tokens. The app is built using open source codes, which have been rigourously tested by the open global community of developers.

2. Are my crypto funds secured in SAS Wallet?

Your funds stored in SAS wallet are completely safe, as long as you have safely kept your seed phrase or private key with you. We do not have access to your crypto holdings. If you lose your seed phrase/private key, then you won't be able to access your crypto funds. It is suggested to keep your seed phrase and private safely.

3. How to restore my old wallet in SAS wallet?

To restore your old crypto wallets into SAS wallet, you need either seed phrase or private key of your old wallet.
